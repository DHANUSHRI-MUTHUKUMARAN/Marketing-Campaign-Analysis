advanced
    The advanced analysis shows that campaign performance is primarily influenced by platform choice and cost efficiency (CPC).
    Instagram and Twitter consistently achieve high ROI due to lower costs, 
    while Pinterest underperforms across all conditions due to higher CPC. 
    Other factors such as 
        campaign duration, 
        customer segment, 
        location, and 
        engagement score
    have minimal impact. 
    Budget reallocation simulations further confirm that shifting investment to high-performing platforms 
    can significantly improve revenue. 
    The uniform nature of the results suggests that the dataset may be synthetic.
